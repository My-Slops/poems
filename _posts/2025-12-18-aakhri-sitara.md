## मूल कविता (हिंदी/उर्दू)

जब अँधेरा बहुत था, एक सितारा बाकी था,  
टूटते हौसलों में भी किनारा बाकी था।  
हमने माना कि सफ़र मुश्किल और लंबा है,  
पर दुआओं में अभी तक वो सहारा बाकी था।  

## English Translation

When darkness was vast, one star still remained,  
Even in collapsing courage, a shore still remained.  
We accepted the journey was hard and long,  
Yet in prayers, that support still remained alive.  

## शब्दार्थ (Difficult Words & Meanings)

- **हौसलों:** courage/resolve.
- **किनारा:** shore/refuge.
- **सहारा:** support.

## रूपकात्मक अर्थ (Metaphorical Meaning)

- The last star symbolizes final reserves of faith.
- The shore represents emotional safety amidst overwhelm.
- The poem argues that even minimal hope can sustain endurance.
